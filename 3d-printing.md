<section>
  <h2>What I can help with</h2>
  <p>This service is best for small-to-medium prints (though I can work with you on larger prints), practical prototypes, and quick iteration. If you already have a file, upload it in the form. If you only have a rough idea, describe what it should do and include dimensions, sketches, or reference photos.</p>

<section>
  <h2>How it works</h2>
  <ol class="printing-steps">
    <li>
      <strong>Submit the form.</strong>
      <p>Upload a file or describe what you want printed.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>I review the request.</strong>
      <p>I check printability, size, material assumptions, and likely failure points.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>You get a quote or recommendation.</strong>
      <p>If the part needs design changes, I’ll flag that before printing.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Print and pickup at NS.</strong>
      <p>Turnaround is very fast, since the printer is here on-campus.</p>
    </li>
  </ol>
</section>
